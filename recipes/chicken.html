Lulu's magic grilled chicken marinade mix

Description

Lulu's magic grilled chicken is called magicfor a reason. It is the perfect blend of savory flavors like paprika, oregano, and onion powder. Add to this a hint of white vinegar and you have a dish that will surely quench your tastebuds. Start with gathering the following:

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken

Steps

  1. Step 1: cut and prepare chicken to your liking
  2. Step 2: gather the ingedients and mix in a bowl
  3. Step 3: Add the chicken to the bowl
  4. Step 4: Set the bowl in the fridge for at least 30 minutes
  5. Step 5: Cook on a pan when ready and enjoy!
